**Recommended Textbooks:**
- Faraway, J. J. (2025). *Linear models with R* (3rd ed.). Chapman and Hall/CRC.

- Weisberg, S. (2014). *Applied linear regression* (4th ed.). Wiley.

**Recommended software-related resources:**
- R Core Team (2023) R: A Language and Environment for Statistical Computing. R Foundation for Statistical Computing. [[link](https://cran.r-project.org/)]

- Posit team (2025) RStudio: Integrated Development Environment for R. Posit Software, PBC. [[link](https://posit.co/products/open-source/rstudio/)]

- Wickham et al. (2023). *R for Data Science* (2nd ed.). O'Reilly Media. [[link](https://r4ds.hadley.nz/)]

**Additional Literature:**
- Fieller, N. (2016). *Basics of matrix algebra for statistics with R.* CRC Press.

- McElreath, R. (2020). *Statistical rethinking: A Bayesian course with examples in R and Stan* (2nd ed.). CRC Press.

- Huff, D. (1993). *How to lie with statistics.* W. W. Norton & Company. (Original work published 1954)

**Recommended blogs:**
- Statistical Modeling, Causal Inference, and Social Science. Gelman et al. [[link](https://statmodeling.stat.columbia.edu/)]
